Our Process

 There are more than 5000 learning institutions in the USA. Sport Scholarship USA will target those schools that fit the applicant’s criteria the best

Analysis

Here we analyze the swimmer’s skill set and times and match this to schools where they will have the greatest chance of being part of the team. We also match the academic achievements and study direction to universities and colleges that offer the right courses

Networking

We actively engage with Coaches and teams- looking for the best fit for our candidate. We set up video calls with coaches, registering applicant for SAT’s, manage the registration to NCAA, NJCAA or NAIA and assist with the completion of University and College applications

The Offer

Once offers have been made we start the final stage where we assist with VISA applications, transcript verification, and SEVIS registrations. We help negotiate a substantial scholarship, advise on travel dates, items to take along, and modes of communication etc.
